AI is moving fast, and with that speed comes a new set of terms that many business readers are now hearing for the first time: RAG and MCP. They describe how modern AI systems get better information, connect to business tools, and, in some cases, go beyond answering questions to carrying out work, because customers are no longer asking only for a chatbot.
