In March, Acronis, Seagate and the Utah Jazz teamed up for a two-day event that combined community impact, innovation and collaboration. The event brought together students from the University of Utah’s Women in Computing Club and the Kahlert School of Computing for a hands-on hackathon. Their challenge: design a web application that would be useful for a Utah Jazz fan.
